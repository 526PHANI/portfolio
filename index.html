Some Sample Projects

Mobile Device Usage Analysis

This project analyzes mobile device usage patterns, focusing on app usage time, screen time, battery drain, and app installation across different demographics.

Objectives

To uncover insights into user behavior related to mobile devices, aiding manufacturers and marketers in understanding usage trends across age groups and genders.

Key Insights

  • The majority of users prefer Android devices, with a significant portion using iOS, indicating a dominant market presence for Android.
  • The distribution of device models shows that the Xiaomi Mi 11 and iPhone 12 are the most popular among users, highlighting brand preference.
  • Battery drain varies slightly across models, with the iPhone 12 showing the highest average consumption, suggesting its potential impact on user experience.
  • Screen on time shows a positive trend with age, peaking in the 30-40 age range, indicating greater engagement among these users.
  • Data usage patterns reveal differences between genders, with females generally consuming more data, particularly in the younger age groups.
  • The number of apps installed fluctuates across age groups, peaking in middle age, suggesting tech-savvy behavior among this demographic.
